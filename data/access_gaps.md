## Region 1: Rural West Virginia

- **Population:** ~1.77 million | **Rural rate:** ~63%
- **Key issue:** Highest drug-overdose death rate → elevated transfemoral amputation rates
- ~40-60 licensed prosthetists statewide; concentrated in Charleston, Huntington, Morgantown
- Rural counties (Logan, Mingo, McDowell, Boone) have zero providers
- Average travel: 45-90+ minutes
- **Coverage: CRITICAL**

## Region 2: Eastern Kentucky

- **Population:** ~750,000 | **Rural rate:** ~75% | **Poverty:** ~25%
- ~15-25 licensed prosthetists; concentrated in Lexington (2+ hours away)
- No providers in Breathitt, Knott, Leslie, Letcher, Pike counties
- Average travel: 60-150+ minutes
- **Coverage: CRITICAL**

## Region 3: Mississippi Delta

- **Population:** ~1.2M across 22 counties | **Poverty:** ~28%
- ~10-20 licensed prosthetists; mostly in Memphis, TN (cross-state barriers)
- Zero providers in Bolivar, Coahoma, Humphreys, Sharkey, Washington, Quitman, Tallahatchie
- Average travel: 60-180+ minutes
- **Coverage: CRITICAL**

*Source: HRSA HPSA, NPI Registry, Census ACS, state health departments*
